Notes & suggestions

  • Your home screen has empty corners. I moved Quests to a fixed top-left badge and the Wish balance top-right so they read as the persistent bar — and kept Inventory / Fairy Log / Me as the bottom nav.
  • The fountain tiers grow + the yellow glow intensifies with level — that's the "reflects current level" cue. Worth pairing with a small "Lv 10 → 11" progress sliver near the Enhance button.
  • On Toss, I expanded your ♥100 + into an amount stepper and a slider, plus a live rarity-odds preview, since "odds shown" is core to the decision. Confirm flows straight into the arrival screen.
  • Quests need a clear done-state: tap a card → it dims, strikes through, the heart fills, and the reward animates up into the top balance. The honesty/verify prompt can be a quick confirm sheet before that.
  • One thing to decide: is Enhance a separate flow from leveling via tosses? If Wishes both summon fairies and upgrade the fountain, players will feel the tension — good, but flag the cost clearly.
